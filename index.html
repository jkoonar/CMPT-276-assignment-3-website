Guitars Made Easy

This website provides details on three types of guitars. This info is meant for people who are interested in purchasing a guitar, and that do not have much knowledge on the instrument. The three types of guitar covered will be Gibson guitars, Fender guitars, and an acoustic guitar brand. Links to the websites of these brands will be provided to the user so that they can obtain more information.

Gibson Stratocaster

These guitars often have a solid body. They are shorter in length relative to Fender telecasters. They often have humbucker pickups, which in turn generate a warmer and thicker guitar tone. These guitars are great if you're interested in playing metal, rock, and the blues.

Visit Gibson Home Page View stratocasters

Fender Telecaster

Possess a solid body. They are longer in length relative to Gibson stratocasters. They have single-coil pickups which generate a brighter sound than that of their Gibson counterparts. These are great for rock, blues, and country music.

Yamaha acoustic

A very popular acoustic guitar. Is a cheaper alternative to an electric guitar. The downside is learning to play with thicker stringers which can be a challenge. These come in various styles and yamaha acoustics have different tonewoods like spruce, mahogany, and rosewood. These in turn generate different tones.
